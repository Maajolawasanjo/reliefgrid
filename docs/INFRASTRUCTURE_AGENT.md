# 🏗️ Infrastructure Safety Agent Specification

## 📌 Role & Responsibility

The **Infrastructure Safety Agent** audits structural integrity across critical civil infrastructure—such as bridges, power grids, water treatment facilities, and road arterial networks—during disaster events.

---

## 🛠️ Capabilities & Output Controls
* Integrates bridge risk ratings and structural vulnerability records.
* Automatically marks damaged bridges or flooded underpasses as impassable in the GIS routing matrix.
