# ⛺ Shelter Capacity Agent Specification

## 📌 Role & Responsibility

The **Shelter Capacity Agent** manages evacuation centers, temporary housing hubs, food supply rations, water purification units, and sanitation logistics for displaced civilian populations.

---

## 🛠️ Automated Operations
* Calculates food and clean water supply depletion timelines based on active occupancy.
* Triggers shelter expansion alerts when capacity crosses 85%.
